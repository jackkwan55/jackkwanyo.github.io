Understanding Gunner

Over the years, Gunner has had many passions. Skateboarding, ping-pong, parkour, and gun-wielding. This kid has gone through a plethora of mind sets, and wouldn't be where he is today without them. Each passion he has gotten in to has brought him through a particular discipline. Gunner developed strong initiative to practice what ever it was that he was into at that time every day. He has a strong heart, and understands the principle of hard work.

Being the only child with four sisters, Gunner had to immediately learn how to grow up brotherless. No one to beat him down, nothing. His sisters had always been something he struggled with growing up, getting into petty fights, etc. But Gunner grew to respect his sisters and care for them deeply. Along with his sisters Gunner also had his most beloved dog, Nonnie. Nonnie has been an especially important part of Gunner's life. Always there for him in times of cuddles and by his bed when he is sleeping at night.

As you can see from Gunner's early age he has always been a crazy fun kid. Spinning on his head, jumping around the house, and doing whatever action packed thing he wanted to do. Gunner was born this way.
